Every file contains a 10 x 4096 array. This corresponds to 10 layers and 4096 being the dimension of the residual stream ($d_{model}$) of Mistral-7B-Instruct-v0.3.
Every file corresponds to one steering direction.
The "\_n" extension denotes that the mean vectors of $A_+$ and $A_-$  are normalized, before subtraction.
